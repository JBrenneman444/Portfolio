About Local Places With
What is Local Places With?

Local Places With is a map of places with fun amenities like bar sports, live entertainment, and games.

How do you define the amenities?
  • Bar Sports: A game that is much more likely to be found in a bar than anywhere else, and usually involves some amount of athletic skill (to be 'good', that is).
  • Live Entertainment: Social and musical activities centered around live entertainment or group participation.
  • Games: Not quite a sport, not quite a social event, but just as fun.

What do I use Local Places With for?

That's up to you! But we're guessing our website would be helpful to people trying to find stuff like this:

  • Bars with pool tables near me or places to play pool near me.
  • Places with live music near me or bars with karaoke near me.
  • Bars with board games near me or bars with trivia nights near me.

Who finds all these amenities?

You do!
